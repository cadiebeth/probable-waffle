My short life as a waffle

By Zackery Ledman

    Griddled warmer's
    Brown my liquid bottom,
    Blanketing me next
    In buttery-syrup sodom'.
    My life
    Expectancy is only going
    On the 20th minute,
    I'll soon be crushed up
    with in the jowls and
    mouth-enamels,
    within it.
